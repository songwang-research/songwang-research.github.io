Thank you for visiting my homepage.

I am Song Wang, a Ph.D. student in the [Namba Laboratory](http://www.icsd2.tj.chiba-u.jp/) at [Chiba University](https://www.chiba-u.ac.jp/), supervised by Associate Professor [Kazuteru Namba](https://researchmap.jp/nambakazuteru?lang=ja), and my research focuses on dependable computing.
